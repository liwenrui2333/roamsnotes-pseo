## When to use this tool

Use it when you know you want a reading but are not sure which *type* fits — a written tarot report, an audio session, or a birth chart reading — or how much to spend. Matching the topic, format, and budget up front stops you from overpaying for a format you will not revisit, and it narrows thousands of Fiverr gigs down to the handful that actually suit your need.

## How to use it

Choose your topic (love, career, or astrology), the delivery format you would genuinely use, and a realistic budget. The matcher suggests a reader type and a budget-appropriate approach, then offers a link to matching readers. As a rough guide: under $20 points you toward short written packages; $20-$45 opens up multi-card spreads and audio; higher budgets suit detailed reports.

## Example

Selecting *Love + written + $20* suggests a relationship-focused tarot reader, a short fixed-price written package, and a search filtered to love tarot readers. You stay in control of the final choice — the tool narrows the field, it does not pick a seller for you.

## FAQ

**Does this book a reader for me?** No. It recommends a reader *type* and links you to matching gigs. You compare reviews and order yourself.

**Why ask about budget?** Format and depth drive price. Matching budget to format keeps you from buying a $90 package when a $20 one answers your question.

**Is one format more accurate?** No. Written reports are easier to compare and re-read; audio and video feel more personal. Accuracy depends on the reader, not the format.
