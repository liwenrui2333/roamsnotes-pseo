## When to use this tool

Use it before you shop so you walk in with a realistic number instead of guessing. Astrology readings span $10 to $150 depending on depth and format, and that range makes it easy to overpay. A budget anchor helps you spot gigs that are suspiciously cheap (and likely templated) or padded with add-ons.

## How to use it

Pick the reading depth and delivery speed. A **short overview** anchors near $15, a **standard birth chart reading** near $45, and a **detailed chart or compatibility reading** near $85; faster turnaround adds roughly $15. Treat the figure as a comparison anchor for browsing, not a fixed market price — then sort gigs around that number.

## Example

Choosing *Standard reading + faster delivery* estimates about $60 and links you to astrology readers near that budget. Use it to judge whether a given gig is fairly priced for what it includes, not as a quote.

## FAQ

**Is this an official price?** No. It is a planning anchor based on common Fiverr ranges. Actual prices vary by seller, depth, and format.

**Why do detailed readings cost more?** They need more birth data and analysis time — natal plus transits, or two charts for compatibility — so they sit at the top of the range.

**Does a higher price mean a better reading?** No. Price reflects depth and format, not accuracy. A clear scope and recent, detailed reviews matter more than the headline number.
